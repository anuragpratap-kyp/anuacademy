About Our Portal

Empowering BSc students with smart semester-wise practice and performance tracking.

Who We Are

This portal is designed to help BSc students prepare effectively for semester exams. Students can practice subject-wise MCQs, track their performance, and improve their scores with structured learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple, clean and efficient online platform where students can test their knowledge anytime and anywhere.

Semester-wise MCQs

Practice questions organized by semester for better preparation.

Performance Tracking

Monitor your MCQ attempts and average score easily.

Easy to Use

Clean interface designed for smooth and distraction-free learning.

About Me

Hi, I'm Anurag, a BSc student passionate about technology and education. I created Anurag Academy to help students prepare for exams through accessible, bilingual MCQs. With skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python (Flask), I enjoy building practical web projects that make learning easier.

My mission is to provide simple, effective, and inclusive tools for exam preparation. I believe education should be accessible to everyone, whether in English or Hindi. This platform is a reflection of my journey as a learner and my commitment to helping peers succeed.

Anurag Academy is more than just a website - it's a community-driven effort to make exam prep smarter, inclusive, and engaging.
